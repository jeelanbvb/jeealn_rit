About The Team

Mr.Ramnath Prabhu was the founder of R.M.Prabhu & co. He was born on February 17 1929 in Kumta. He completed his education from Kumta. He founded R.M.Prabhu & co. 40 years ago in 1976. He started with a small shop way back in 70's which has now grown up to be such a huge company due to his and his children's hardwork. He was the owner of this company until his death in 2014 after which his eldest son Mr. Krishnakant Prabhu and the youngest son Mr. Brijesh Prabhu have been continuing this company as owners.
